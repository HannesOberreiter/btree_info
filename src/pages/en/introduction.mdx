### B.TREE - proudly serving since 2014

This beekeeping web application was made to help beekeepers manager their tasks and have an quick overview. You add your feedings, harvests, treatments, checkups and much more. Move hive/colonies between apiaries and also manage your bee queens on your colonies. You can create your own rearing methods and edit most options to your needs (eg. hive type, feeding type, treatment methods, ...). Apiaries are easily managed with a apiary map which features satellite images and weather overlays for premium users.

In our beekeeping app most of the data is shown in tables for better overview of big data, also you can export your data as CSV for save keeping of the data yourself and optionally generate a full database backup with all your saved data. An interactive calendar on the dashboard helps to keep an overview over your past task and upcoming beekeeping events. Premium user can also access the API which allows them to export the iCal events and have access to helpful and responsive statistic in our beekeeping software.

The web app is made for hobby and professional beekeepers alike, but the main focus was to build an app for organic beekeeper who must keep records or beekeepers who like to to keep record and manage their beekeeping task in the cloud with it advantages. As it allows multiple users to work simultanious and to work in field and at home without data syncing needs.
